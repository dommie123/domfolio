About Me

I have worked on several projects for Bank of America in the two years I have worked for them. Many of these projects focused on developing the UI for several programs, including a data cache, event signaller, margin viewer, and most recently, a predictive monitoring system for all of the bank's applications! As a game developer, however, I have worked on several smaller games for various game jams and bootcamp courses as well as one larger game, which will be released later this year! For more information on the game jams I have taken part in, as well as any updates on future projects I make, I recommend visiting my itch page here.


Despite my professional experience, I don't just make games and software. I also enjoy playing videogames. Some of my favorites include Minecraft and Super Smash Brothers Ultimate. When it comes to multiplayer, I also play Overwatch 2, Sea of Thieves, Halo Infinite, and a handful of smaller indie titles depending on what everyone else is playing at the time. As for single player, I like to play whatever game I want (currently No Man's Sky). I could go on about what games I play, but I'll leave it at that in the interest of page length. For many of the games I have listed, I like to play them on PC, however every once in a while, I like to play games on Nintendo Switch, 3DS, and Playstation 5. One last thing to note is I will also stream or post videos about the games I play. You can check them out for yourself by visiting my YouTube page.
