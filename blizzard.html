About

Blizzard the Runner is a 2D endless runner/platformer with a big emphasis on using a grapple hook to traverse the world. It stars Blizzard, a talking husky with a love for adventure and treasure hunting, and a character I have conceived back in 2007 when I first got him as a plushie. In this game, he has traversed all the chasms of an ancient desert ruin in the outskirts of Egypt to claim a secret treasure that has rumored to exist for centuries. Little does he know, the treasure is protected by an ancient desert guardian, a golem known as the "Sand Titan". After awakening this guardian, Blizzard must escape the desert and run as far as possible to avoid the guardian's wrath. The desert is not a friendly environment, however. He will need to dodge various obstacles that block his path, including cacti, vultures, and ruined buildings from the ancient city that holds the temple. Will you escape the desert and claim the treasure? Click the link and download my game now to find out!
